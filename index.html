About

Dr. Ragel was appointed a Senior Lecturer in Computer Engineering at the University of Peradeniya in December 2007 and has been a Professor since October 2017. He has been a Professional Member of the IEEE and IEEE Computer Society since 2005 and a Senior Member since 2014.

Prof Ragel has co-authored over 200 peer-reviewed articles on Internet of Things, Wearable Computing, Bioinformatics, and Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. He has won several best paper awards, the most prolific Sri Lankan author award from Elsevier Research in 2017, and Presidential awards for scientific publication in Sri Lanka in 2018 and 2023.

He was named the top scientist in computer science in Sri Lanka by the AD Scientific Index from 2021 to 2025. He won the National Educator Gold Awards 2024 for Computing. Prof Ragel received the 2025 IEEE Computer Society Mary Kenneth Keller Computer Science & Engineering Undergraduate Teaching Award, the first academic to receive this award from the Global South.
